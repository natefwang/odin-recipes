Foolproof Eggs Benedict

Perfectly poached eggs and a no-fail hollandaise sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 8 slices Canadian bacon
  • 4 buttered and toasted English muffins
  • 8 poached eggs
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Chives
  • Hollandaise:
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tsp water
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 stick unsalted buter

Recipe instructions

Making the hollandaise sauce
  1. Combine egg yolk, water, lemon juice, and apinch of salt in the bottom of a cup that barely fits the head of an immersion blender. Melt butter in a small suacepan ove high heat, swirling constantly, until foaming subsides. Transfer butter toa 1 cup liquid measuring cup.
  2. Place head of immersion blender into the bottom of the cup and turn it on. With the blender on, slowly pour hot butter into cup. Contents should emulsify within the cup. Season to taste and serve immediately
Putting it all together
  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat and cook Canadian bacon until crispy golden brown
  2. Cut English muffin into halves. Place 1 ham slice on each muffin half and top with one poached egg. Season with salt and pepper
  3. Spoon hollandaise sauce over each egg. Sprinkle with chives for garnish
